Recipes:

Caprese Salad

Traditionally, the caprese salad calls for sliced tomatoes and slices of mozzarella. This version uses cherry tomatoes, and either mozzarella balls or small cubes of the cheese. This gives you a salad that's a bit more like a salad, and works better as a side item.

Loco Moco

Loco moco is a dish featured in contemporary Hawaiian cuisine. There are many variations, but the traditional loco moco consists of white rice, topped with a hamburger, a fried egg, and brown gravy.

Pasta Primavera

Pasta Primavera isn’t just for spring and summer, this is a veggie packed pasta dish that’s perfect year round! It has a refreshing and satisfying flavor and it is a versatile recipe so you can add different kinds of vegetables you might already have on hand.
