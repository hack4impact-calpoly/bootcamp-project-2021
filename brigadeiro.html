Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro is every child's favorite treat at Brazilian birthday parties. This is a pretty quick and easy recipe and only requires two main ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Condensed Milk
  • Chocolate of choice
  • Sprinkles of choice

  1. Chop the chocolate into smaller pieces if necessary.
  2. Pour the condensed milk and the chocolate into a pan and keep on medium high heat
  3. Continuously stir, making sure that the contents don't stick to the bottom of the pan
  4. When the paste stops sticking to the bottom of the pan, turn of the heat, and let the brigadeiro cool
  5. Once cooled, mold into small spheres and roll them on sprinkles. Spread butter on hands to avoid sticking.
