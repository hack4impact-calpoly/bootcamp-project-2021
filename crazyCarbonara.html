Crazy Carbonara

Don't let this fancy italian dish fool you! This is an easy recipe for a nice dinner at home to impress your friends

Pick your favorite pasta shape, mine are rigatoni and farefelli! Pair this lovely meal with a nice white wine :)

Here are the Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Pasta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3 oz Parmesan
  • 1/4 lb Bacon
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Here are the Instructions:

  1. First begin by bringing a pot of salted water to a boil and add in the pasta.
  2. Scrabble eggs in a large bowl and mix in the parmesan
  3. In a large pan cook diced bacon until beginning to brown. Add in diced garlic until fragrent
  4. Add in the wine and simmer until 1/2 the liquid cooks off
  5. Once pasta is done, add the pasta to the egg mixture. Then add in the bacon
  6. Salt to taste!
