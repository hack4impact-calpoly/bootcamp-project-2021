Grilled Beef Shawarma Bowl

Beef shawarma is one of my favorite go-to Middle Eastern recipes that’s usually on every middle eastern restaurant menu and famous street food around the world. It is one of those recipes that can easily be recreated and enjoyed at home too. Make this beef shawarma into a bowl with salad, rice, or a sandwich. The result will be a juicy, tangy beef over rice with some of your favorite veggies of choice.

Ingredients You'll Need

  • Ground cumin
  • Ground coriander
  • Smoked paprika
  • Ground tumeric
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground cloves
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Beefsteak, or your favorite cut
  • Olive oil

Make It Your Own!



How To Make

  1. Combine all the spices in a small bowl and mix well.
  2. Place the beef steak in a shallow dish and sprinkle with the seasoning mix. Drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil. Rub it evenly on all sides of the meat.
  3. Cover and allow it to sit for 15 to 30 minutes at room temp.
  4. Heat an outdoor grill or grill pan over medium-high, and brush with some oil.
  5. Add marinated steak and grill for 2-4 minutes per side, or until it’s done to your liking.
  6. Allow the steak to sit for a bit before slicing it.
  7. Add a base of cooked basmati rice to each bowl, and top with chopped veggies, pickles, and sliced shawarma steak. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve with yogurt and pita bread.
