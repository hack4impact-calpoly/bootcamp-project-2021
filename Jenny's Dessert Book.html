Welcome to Jenny's Dessert Recipe Book!

These sweet treats have beginner friendly recipes and even experienced bakers can have fun and challenge themselves with modifications. Whenever you want to impress some friends or make a treat to satisfy your sweet tooth, look no further than Jenny's Dessert Recipes.

Recipes Below

Banana Bread

Unlike regular loafed banana bread, these are a brownie shaped rendition created for sharing at potlucks and around the dinner table. These require a lot of bananas for that moist texture so make sure to stock up.


Snickerdoodle

Snickerdoodles are great for beginner bakers to test out their skills. With its cinnamon coating and soft insides, these cookies should be a favorite wherever they go.


Ice Cream

Craving Ice Cream but don't want to leave the house? Our homemade vanilla ice cream solves that with a basic recipe that anyone can follow along.
