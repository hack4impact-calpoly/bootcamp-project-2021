Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is Indonesian fried rice. It's not your typical fried rice from the local Chinese restaurant. You can put anything you want as toppings but this recipe only includes my favorites. This recipe was inspired by my dad's recipe. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

Note: There are no measurements on purpose. All ingredients are to the cook's discretion and a lot of taste testing!

  • White rice
  • Indonesian Kecap Manis
  • White Pepper
  • Knorr Chicken Bouillon
  • Eggs
  • Spam
  • Carrots
  • Green Onions

Instructions

  1. Cook desired amount of rice. Use about 20% less water than usual.
  2. Once rice is done cooking, let it sit for four hours.
  3. Cut carrots and green onions to small pieces.
  4. Cube cut the spam.
  5. Once rice is done sitting, beat and cook eggs in a large wok. It is important not to scramble the eggs too much when cooking initially.
  6. When eggs are almost done cooking, put all the vegetables and spam into the wok.
  7. Put a pinch of white pepper and chicken bouillon for every cup of rice you cooked into the wok.
  8. Stir ingredients until slightly charred.
  9. Put rice into wok.
  10. Mix while adding kecap manis until rice is a medium brown.
  11. Taste test and add whatever you feel like is lacking.
  12. Done!
