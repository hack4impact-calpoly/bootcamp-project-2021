Blueberry Muffins

These blueberry oatmeal muffins are packed with flavor, loaded with fresh blueberries, and made with oat flour, maple syrup, and no butter. They come out fluffy and so tasty. Hard to believe that they are made with no refined sugar, no butter, and no flour. Also, the flour used is simply oat flour which you can make on your own using rolled oats! You will love these healthy blueberry oatmeal muffins which can be enjoyed as a quick on-the-go breakfast, a snack, or a healthy dessert option.

Ingredients You'll Need

  • 1 1/2 cups oat flour
  • 1/4 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 large eggs at room temperature
  • Â¾ cup milk of choice
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil melted
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Make It Your Own!



How To Make

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F and line a muffin tin with 12 cup liners.
  2. To a large bowl, add the flour, rolled oats, baking powder, ground cinnamon, and salt. Mix well to combine.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk in the eggs, milk, maple syrup, coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Next, add the egg and milk mixture to the oat flour mixture. Whisk to combine.
  5. Fold in the fresh blueberries and divide the batter among your prepared muffin cups.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let the muffins cool for at least 5 minutes before serving.
