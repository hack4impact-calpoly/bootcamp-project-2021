Avocado Almond Butter Smoothie

A healthy smoothie that tastes like a milkshake and is high in nutrition!

Ingredients

Serves 1 Person

  • 1 avocado
  • 2 cups of your choice of milk
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 dash of cinnamon
  • 1 -2 tbsp maple syrup or honey

Optional Ingredients

  • protein powder - great for an afterworkout smoothie!
  • greek yogurt - if you want a creamier smoothie!

Preparation

Prep Time: < 5 minutes

  1. Turn on the blender.
  2. Throw everything into the blender.
  3. Blend!
