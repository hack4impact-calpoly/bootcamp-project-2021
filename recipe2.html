Pork Siu Mai

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb pork
  • 10 jumbo shrimps - 1/2 minced, 1/2 chopped roughly
  • 2 T light soy
  • 1 T dark soy
  • 1.5 T fish sauce
  • 3/4 T garlic
  • 1/2 T white pepper
  • 1 T. Cornstarch
  • 1tsp sesame oil
  • 2tsp sugar
  • Round gyoza wrappers

Preparation

  1. Combine all of above except roughly chopped shrimp.
  2. Mix thoroughly, kneeding pork mixture in between fingers for 5 mins until smooth and sticky. Add roughly chopped shrimp last.
  3. Form with round gyoza wrappers between pointer finger and thumb.
  4. Steam on medium heat for 10 mins
