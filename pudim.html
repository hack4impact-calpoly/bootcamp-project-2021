Pudim

This is a simpler version of what many people know as flan.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • hot water
  • condensed milk
  • milk
  • eggs

  1. Melt the sugar in a large pan and cook until golden.
  2. Add the warm water and stir
  3. Let the solution boil until the sugar is dissolved and the liquid thickens.
  4. pour the syrup in the selected baking tin for the pudim
  5. Blend the condensed milk, milk, and eggs in blender and place in the baking tin with the syrup.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and cook it bain-marie in the oven at medium heat for 1 hour and 30 mins.
  7. Once ready let cool and leave in the refrigerator for 6 hours.
  8. It is ready to serve.
