This vanilla ice cream recipe can be modified to whatever flavor you like. Just replace the vanilla extract with other flavored extracts. To step it up a notch, mix in fresh sliced fruit after the ice cream is complete. This ice cream recipe works best with an ice cream maker that can churn the chilled mixture. Although, this is a great arm exercise for those who don't own an ice cream machine. Stay tuned for other ice cream recipes that don't use an ice cream machine.


Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups heavy cream
  • 1¼ cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, add 1 cup of heavy cream and add sugar, salt, and vanilla
  2. Put saucepan over meadium heat until the sugar dissolves
  3. Put in fridge until chilled
  4. Whenever you want to eat, mix until an ice cream texture is achieved
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
