Combo 'za


The most delicious pizza I've ever eaten. Serves 2 hungry people

Pizza is one of the most sought after Italian dishes and is one you can easily prepare at home for you and all your guests to enjoy. While making the perfect pizza can sometimes be intimidating, this recipe offers you step by step details to ensure the most delicious, fresh pizza base.

The fresh pizza base topped with perfectly baked mushrooms, peppers, tomatoes, and onions molded in with a healthy heap of gooey cheese makes this one of my favorite dishes - and it'll soon be yours too!


Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 100 mL tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onions
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 100g processed cheese
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1/2 green pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 tbsp dry yeast
  • water as required

Instructions

  1. Add all purpose flour, salt, baking powder, 1 tsp oil to a dough kneading plate
  2. Take a little warm water, add the yeast and sugar to it and set aside for 10-15 minutes to let yeast rise
  3. Once the yeast has risen, add it to the dough with some water and set aside for 4-6 hours
  4. Preheat oven to 355 degrees Farenheit. Dust space using dry flour and roll dough into circular base. Add to oven for 10 minutes
  5. Wash the green peppers and slice it thinly. Peel and cut thin slices of onions and add both to bowl
  6. In the same bowl, add chopped tomatoes and mushrooms and grate processed and mozzarella cheese in
  7. Spread tomato ketchup all over the pizza base, evenly put veggies all around, and add a thick layer of cheese
  8. Set oven to 480 degrees Farenheit and bake for 10 minutes. Add oregano and chili flakes to taste and enjoy!
