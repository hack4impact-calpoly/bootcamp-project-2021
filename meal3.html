Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

A warm, creamy soup that is easy and cheap to make!

Ingredients

Serves 1 Person

  • 3 tbps olive oil
  • 2 red onions, chopped
  • 3 gloves minced garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cus heavy cream
  • 1 bunch of basil
  • kosher salt
  • ground pepper
  • parmesan cheese

Add an Ingredient



Preparation

Prep Time: < 25 minutes

  1. Heat the olive oil over a medium heat in a large pot, and season the onions with kosher salt. Stir the onions until it turns golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and stir for 1 more minute.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes and chicken stock.
  4. Add in pepper and bring the soup to a boil.
  5. Stir in cream and add basil.
  6. Simmer for about 15 minutes.
  7. Serve!
