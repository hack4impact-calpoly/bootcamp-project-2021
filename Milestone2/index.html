In this website you will find three of my favorite Indonesian dishes! Check out the recipes down below. Enjoy!

Recipes

Nasi Goreng

Nasi Goreng is Indonesian fried rice. It's not your typical fried rice from the local Chinese restaurant. You can put anything you want as toppings but this recipe only includes my favorites.

Bihun goreng

Bihun goreng is the Indonesian version of chow mian. It features thin rice noodles along with a blend of vegetable and meat toppings.

Siomay

Siomay is another classic Indonesian dish. They are similar to dumplings. What makes them really good is the peanut sauce. Those with peanut allergies beware!
