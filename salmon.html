Creamy Tuscan Salmon


An absolutely exquisite salmon dish. Serves 3

Salmon can sometimes be intimidating to cook trying to find the perfect blend between too pink and overdone. However, with this simple recipe, your salmon is guaranteed to come out perfect every single time.

The well cooked salmon basted in the delicious sauce is sure to satisfy even the pickiest of palates. If you are looking for a good salmon dish, look no further.


Ingredients

  • 3 salmon fillets
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 1/3 cup vegetable broth
  • 5 oz jarred sun dried tomato in oil
  • 1 3/4 cups heavy cream
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 3 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Season salmon filets on both sides with salt and pepper and sear in hot pan for 5 minutes on each side
  2. In same pan, melt the butter in the cooking juices. Add in garlic and onion and stir fry until translucent
  3. Add sun dried tomatoes and let fry for 2 minutes and then pour in vegetable broth, allowing sauce to reduce slightly
  4. Reduce heat to low, add heavy cream, and brimg to gentle simmer. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add in baby spinach until cooked and add parmesan cheese. Cook for another minute
  6. Add the grilled salmon fillet back into pan, sprinkle with parsley, and baste/li>
  7. Serve and enjoy!
