My Cooking Experience

1-2 Years

I have always grew up in a household where my family cooks for each other. My mom cooked a lot, so I would just watch her cook for the family. The food was always good, but I was never given the opportunity to learn until I was a lot older in high school when my mom could trust me with the kitchen. Ever since then, I have been cooking on a weekly basis, learning from Youtube videos or winging recipes from my mind.


Some of my favorite include (which can be found on this list) Xiao Long Bao, Mom's Fired Rice, Classic (Personal) Breakfast, and Gourmet Instant Ramen. A lot of these food items are convenient to me as a former high school student, but also are significant to me as these are the foods that started my "foodie" lifestyle.
