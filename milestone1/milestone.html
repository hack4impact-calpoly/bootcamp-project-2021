Welcome to Bites with Jacqueline!

During the COVID-19 pandemic, many of us were in quarantine and found ourselves with some free time in our hands. For me, I used that time to learn how to cook.

I can safely say that after more than a year in quarantine, it's incredible to discover how cooking is not something that I can easily master. There are some people who can pick up cooking as if they were born with the skill and some people who need a lot of time and practice to pick up cooking. I fall in the latter.

That's why I created this page to share foolproof recipes that even beginners can't mess up on! Go ahead and take a look at easy recipes to make down below.


Easy Bites

Kimchi Fried Rice

Spicy kimchi. Juicy veggies. Steaming rice. Kimchi fried rice is a heartwarming Korean dish that can be filling, savory, and easy to make.

Vietnamese Spring Rolls

Crisp veggies. Juicy protein. All wrapped up in a rice paper bundle. These spring rolls are a refreshing meal to have dipped in classic Vietnamese sauces.

Vermicelli Noodle Bowl

Rice noodles. Colorful veggies. Bursting flavors. This light, flavorful bowl is a common Vietnamese dish that can be easily made in less than 30 minutes.
