Grilled Cheese

A spin on the standard grilled cheese.

Traditionally, grilled cheese calls for bread, butter, and cheese. This version uses garlic and parmesean seasoning, combined with a culmination of cheeses to create an intricate blend of the "perfect bite". While it's not the most healthy foods reccomended, it's an absolute must on cheat days! Note, there is enough cheese in this recipie so if you really think about it...you're getting your protein for the day:)

The recipe below is supposed to act as a template, please feel free to add/remove ingredients as you'd like!

Ingredients

Serves 1 person

  • 4 Tbsp. unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2 Tbsp. finely chopped parsley
  • 1/2 garlic clove, finely grated
  • 1 slice of parmesean cheese
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of your choice of bread
  • 1 Tbsp. parmesean and garlic seasoning

Preparation

  1. Pepare the parsley and garlic as instructed and set aside
  2. In a pan, apply butter to both sides of the bread
  3. Let it sit on medium heat while you add in garlic and cheese slices
  4. Sprinkle in parsley once the cheese starts to melt
  5. Flip bread to the other side to ensure both sides are cooked well
  6. Sprinkle parmesean and garlic seasoning on the top
  7. Enjoy! I hope you like it!
