Spaghetti Squash

            
A spin on the standard pasta.


              Spaghetti Squash is a great alternative to using regular pasta. 
          While there is only one way to actually cook the squash, the dishes you can make with the cooked "pasta" are endless. 
          A perfect meal on a busy night!


               The recipe below is supposed to act as a template, please feel free to add/remove ingredients as you'd like! 
            

          

          
        

        
Ingredients

        
    
         
  • 1 spaghetti squash
    • 
        
  • 4 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
    • 
        
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

        
    
           
  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Farenheit.
    2. 
           
  2. Slice the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and scoop out seeds and ribbings.
    3. 
           
  3. Drizzle the inside of the squash with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
    4. 
           
  4. Place the spaghetti squash cut side down on the baking sheet and use a fork to poke holes. Roast for 30 to 40 minutes or until lightly browned on the outside, fork tender, but still a litte bit firm. 
    5. 
           
  5. Remove from the oven and flip the squash so that itâ€™s cut side up. When cool to the touch, use a fork to scrape and fluff the strands from the sides of the squash.
    6. 
           
  6. Mix in your favorite pasta sauce or eat as it is!
    7. 
           
  7. Enjoy! I hope you like it!
