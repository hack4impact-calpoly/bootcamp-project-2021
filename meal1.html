Banana Peanut Butter Smoothie

A quick, easy smoothie that is high in fiber, potassium, and vitamins B6 and C!

Ingredients

Serves 1 Person

  • 1 banana
  • 2 cups of your choice of milk
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 dash of cinnamon

Optional Ingredients

  • protein powder - great for an afterworkout smoothie!
  • greek yogurt - if you want a creamier smoothie!

Add an Ingredient



Preparation

Prep Time: < 5 minutes

  1. Turn on the blender.
  2. Throw everything into the blender.
  3. Blend!
