Carbonara (Roma)


You're going to thank me for introducing you to the TASTIEST meal you will ever have the pleasure of experiencing.


During my short time in Rome, I've been blessed to experience some of the best Italian food around. And thus far, Carbonara holds the crown. A simple recipe, Carbonara is a combination of pasta, egg, cheese and pancetta. I know! So Simple! Yet soooo tasty. While I'm only an expert in eating carbonara, I'll outline the recommended way to make this delicious meal at home.

Ingredients

Serving Size: 2 People


  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves of peeled garlic
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Ingredient:


Method

  1. Boil water in large saucepan
  2. Chop pancetta and grate cheese, mix together
  3. Beat eggs and set aside
  4. Add 1 tsp salt to water, add spaghetti and cook for 10 minutes
  5. Fry pancetta with garlic and butter for 5 minutes
  6. Mix eggs and cheese
  7. Pour eggs and cheese into pan with spaghetti and pancetta
  8. Cook until pasta is coated
  9. Enjoy!
