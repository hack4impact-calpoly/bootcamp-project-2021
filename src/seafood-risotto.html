Seafood Risotto (Anzio)


I don't think I will ever make this dish on my own because the ingredients are pretty pricey, but this can serve as a guide to what goes into making this delicious dish. I have only had seafood risotto once in my life, but it was life changing. Given, it was made by chefs at the top of their field with some of the best ingredients available, but if you or I can come close to replicating this I'd say that's a win.


This dish requires a lot of prep/cook time, so it won't serve as a quick meal. But it'll be a good challenge if you're bored from cooking the simple meals of a college student. If you love all kinds of seafood, this is definitely the dish for you!

Ingredients

Serving Size: 2 People

  • 1 lb prawns
  • 36 small mussels
  • 50g squid
  • 50g butter
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 350g risotto rice
  • 25g parmesan
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Peel prawns, cook seafood in large saucepan
  2. Melt butter in large pan, add shallots, salt and risotto rice
  3. Cook risotto for 20 minutes on medium-low heat
  4. Add seafood to risotto to allow absorption of flavors
  5. Enjoy!
