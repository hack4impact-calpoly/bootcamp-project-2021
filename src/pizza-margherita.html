Pizza Margherita (di Napoli)


While I love me some Domino's Pizza at 3 in the morning, nothing in the US can compare to the pizza I've had in Italy. Although pizza margherita is extremely simple in it's ingredients, when cooked correctly, the flavor is out of this world.


The prep time for this dish is a few hours if you include the time needed to allow the dough to rise, but the actual preparation and cook time is pretty quick. Make this meal for yourself on a chilly Saturday night or when you have friends over! I assure you it'll be a hit.

Ingredients

Serving Size: 2 People


  • 1 kg Italian "00" flour
  • 30g fresh yeast
  • 500 ml water
  • 10 g salt
  • 80g olive oil
  • 40 leaves of basil
  • 80g mozzarella cheese
  • 400g canned tomato
  • salt

Add Ingredient



Method

  1. Combine the yeast, salt, warm water in a bowl
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Let the dough rise for 3 hours
  4. Prepare tomato sauce with salt and olive oil
  5. Flatten dough into desired shape
  6. Spread sauce, mozarella and basil over the dough
  7. Bake at 480 degrees F for 5 or 6 minutes
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and enjoy!
