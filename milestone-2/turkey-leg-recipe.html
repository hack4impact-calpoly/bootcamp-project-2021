Fire Nation Turkey Leg Recipe

From Avatar: The Last Airbender

Ingredients

  • 3 large skin-on turkey legs
  • 6 Tbsps butter
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Thai seasoning paste (lemon grass, ginger, chili peppers)
  • 1 tsp red curry paste
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 Tbsps garlic, minced
  • 2 Tbsps brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup golden raisins
  • 2 Tbsps ghee or butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Combine all of the ingredients expect for the ghee/butter to form a sticky marinade. Gently oil the skin away from the meat and slide 3 tablespoons of butter under the skin.
  2. Pour the marinade over the turkey legs and chill for at least 1 hour or overnight. Reserve 3 tablespoons of marinade for basting.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Arrange vegetables in a roasting pan and place the turkey legs side-by-side on top of the veggies. Cover and roast for 20 minutes.
  4. Uncover the turkey legs and spread the remaining marinade over the tops of each leg, then brush the melted ghee over the legs. Return to the oven for an additional 20-30 minutes, or until the legs are cooked through and no longer pink.
  5. Serve alongside a cup of jasmine tea, Zuko’s honor, and enjoy!
