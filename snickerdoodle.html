This snickerdoodle recipe uses a lot of ingredients but the assembly process is very easy. Rolling the dough makes this recipe kid friendly and a nice baking activity with friends. Cream of tartar is typically always used in snickerdoodle recipes, however, if that it the only ingredient you're missing, the recipe will taste very similar without it. This recipe yields around 45 cookies so ratio the ingredients accordingly or if you find yourself with lots of extra batter storing in the freezer is perfect.


Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup shortening
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Add an Ingredient

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F Degrees
  2. Combine wet ingredients in a bowl
  3. Slowly add all the dry ingredients to the same bowl
  4. Form dough into balls
  5. Mix 2 TBSP sugar and cinnamon together
  6. Roll dough balls into sugar mixture
  7. Lay balls onto lined baking tray
  8. Bake for 8-10 min or until set
  9. Serve and Enjoy!
