Grilled Cheese

Traditionally, grilled cheese calls for bread, butter, and cheese. This version uses garlic and parmesean seasoning, combined with a culmination of cheeses to create an intricate blend of the "perfect bite". While it's not the most healthy foods reccomended, it's an absolute must on cheat days!

Spaghetti Squash

Spaghetti Squash is a great alternative to using regular pasta. While there is only one way to actually cook the squash, the dishes you can make with the cooked "pasta" are endless. A perfect meal on a busy night!
