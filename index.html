Taouis=ca

Tapioca is a very simple and filling. You can fill it however you like.

Ingredients

  • Tapioca flour (NOTE: this is different from tapioca starch)
  • Filling of choice

  1. Heat a medium sized frying pan over the stove.
  2. Pour a thin layer of taioca flour onto the pan.
  3. Flip the tapioca to the other once the flour sticks together.
  4. Add filling of choice. This can be ham and cheese, fruit, chocolate, condensed milk or even simply butter.
  5. Either fold or roll the finished tapioca and it is ready to be served.
