Bagel Sandwich

Similar to the Breakfast Burrito, this sandwich is perfect for some morning fuel.

Whats best about this food item is its versatility. Pick your preferred bagel and toppings!

Here are the Ingredients:

  • One everything bagel/li>
  • 1 Avocado
  • Cream Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Tomato
  • Lettuce

Here are the Instructions:

  1. First begin by washing and cutting up all the produce
  2. Toast the bagel in an air fryer or toaster oven
  3. Once the bagel is toasted, spread the cream cheese on the bagel.
  4. Add the avocado, tomato, lettuce, salt, and pepper.
