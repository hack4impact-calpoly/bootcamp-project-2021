Cheese Omelette

As stated by Wikipedia Cheese Omelette, an omelette or omelet is a dish made from beaten eggs, fried with butter or oil in a frying pan (without stirring as in scrambled egg). It is quite common for the omelette to be folded around fillings such as chives, vegetables, mushrooms, meat (often ham or bacon), cheese, or some combination of the above. Whole eggs or egg whites are often beaten with a small amount of milk, cream, or water.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk or as much needed
  • Salt and freshlty ground white pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese

Preparation

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl with a whisk
  2. Add milk and season with salt and white pepper
  3. Whisk for a few minutes until egg mixture is foamy
  4. Melt butter in a small, nonstick skillet over medium-low heat
  5. Pour in egg mixture and twirl skillet so bottom is evenly covered with egg
  6. Cook for 1 minute until egg starts to set
  7. Lift edges with a spatula and tilt the skillet
  8. Repeat until no visible liquid egg remains
  9. Carefully flip omelette and cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute
  10. Sprinkle cheese in one line in the middle of the omelette and fold omelette in half
  11. Cook for 20 seconds, then slide omelette onto plate!
