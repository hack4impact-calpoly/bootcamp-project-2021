The Classic Chicken Burger

As stated by Wikipedia Chicken Sandwhich, a chicken sandwich is a sandwich that typically consists of boneless, skinless chicken breast served between slices of bread, on a bun, or on a roll. Variations on the "chicken sandwich" include the chicken burger or chicken on a bun, hot chicken, and chicken salad sandwich.

Ingredients

Making the Patty from Scratch:

  • 1 pound extra-lean ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup Italian-seasoned bread crubms
  • 1/2 small onion that is finely grated
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic that are minced
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil

Add an Ingredient:



Preparation

  1. Mix ground chicken, 1/4 cup bread crumbs, onion, egg, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a bowl
  2. Moisten hands and shape chicken mixture, 2 tablespoons at a time, into flat, oval-shaped patties
  3. Spread remaining 1/4 cup bread crumbs into a shallow dish
  4. Roll patties in bread crumbs to coat
  5. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  6. Cook patties in hot oil until deep brown in color on the bottom, 5 to 6 minutes
  7. Turn patties and cook until other side is browned, 3 to 4 minutes more
  8. Add other vegetables and add ons such as ketchup, tomatoes, lettuce, onions, etc.!
