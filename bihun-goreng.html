Bihun Goreng

Description

Bihun goreng is the Indonesian version of chow mian. It features thin rice noodles along with a blend of vegetable and meat toppings. This dish is great for dinner and lunch!

Ingredients

Note: There are no measurements on purpose. All ingredients are to the cook's discretion and a lot of taste testing!

  • Cooking oil
  • Sliced Chicken Thigh
  • Eggs
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Rice Vermicelli
  • Bok choy
  • Soy Sauce
  • sesame oil
  • Sugar
  • Indonesian Kecap Manis

Add an Ingredient




Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with one teaspoon of soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar each.
  2. Boil water and boil rice vermicelli for one minute. Strain
  3. Add cooking oil to a sauce pan and cook chicken until 70% cooked.
  4. Add green onions, garlic, and bok choy to pan.
  5. Add eggs and scramble it.
  6. Add rice vermicelli
  7. Mix while adding kecap manis and sesame oil until it reaches the taste you desire!
  8. Done!
