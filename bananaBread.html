This banana is easy to serve and are a smaller portion size than that of typical banana bread. The more bananas that you use, the more moist the bread will be and the bananas used should have lots of brown spots. Although yellow bananas can be used, this recipe tastes best with overripe bananas.


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups ripe bananas (about 3 - 4 medium)
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Add an Ingredient

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F Degrees
  2. Combine all dry ingredients except the brown sugar
  3. In a seperate bowl cream butter and sugar together
  4. Add one egg at a time to the butter sugar mix
  5. Add the bananas and vanilla to the butter sugar mix until combined
  6. Slowly add the dry ingredients
  7. Put in flat brownie pan and bake for 50-60min
  8. enjoy!
